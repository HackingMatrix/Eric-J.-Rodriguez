This is Me

Eric J. Rodriguez's transformative journey began in Ecuador, where a culture shock at the age of eleven fueled a lifelong passion for leveraging technology as a bridge to close gaps and create opportunities.

His pivotal moment in seventh grade, witnessing the introduction of the school's first computer lab, set the stage for a career dedicated to the positive impact of technology. From serving as the Education Regional Director at Intel, influencing lives globally, to his commitment in organizations like Aguila Youth and SHPE, Eric's path reflects resilience, innovation, and an unwavering drive to make a difference.
