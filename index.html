Let's Meet Eric

From his early days as a young boy experiencing a profound cultural shift in Ecuador to becoming a visionary leader in the technology sector, Eric J. Rodriguez's journey has been driven by a deep belief in the transformative power of education and technology. At the age of eleven, when his family moved from Houston to Ecuador, Eric was confronted with a stark contrast between wealth and poverty. This culture shock ignited his lifelong passion for using technology as a bridge to close gaps and create opportunities.
